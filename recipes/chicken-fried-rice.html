Chicken Fried Rice

Description

Chicken Fried Rice is a great dish for bulking and a tasty one too! Here's the right way to make chicken fried rice!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon of water
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 table spoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked with white rice, cold
  • 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. beat egg and water together in a small bowl. Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat; add egg and cook without stirring , 1 to 2 minutes. Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds; set aside
  2. heat oil in the same skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until soft, about three minutes. Stir in rice, chicken, soysauce, and pepper. Cook and stir for about five minutes; stir in shredded egg. Serve hot.
